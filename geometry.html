Optimal Transport & Information Geometry

Information geometry and optimal transport provide geometric tools to study statistical models and learning dynamics. My interests include:

  • Fisher-Rao metrics and dual affine connections
  • Wasserstein distances and geodesic learning paths
  • Applications in variational inference, generative models, and structure learning
