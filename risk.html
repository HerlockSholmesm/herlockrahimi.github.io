Risk-Averse Optimization

Risk-averse optimization tackles decision-making under uncertainty by optimizing for worst-case or tail-end outcomes. I investigate:

  • CVaR and upper quantile-based objectives
  • Distributionally robust optimization (DRO) using f-divergence and Wasserstein metrics
  • Applications in federated learning and portfolio optimization
